About Me

I am a dedicated Salesforce Administrator with over 5 years of extensive experience in the healthcare sector. My journey from Medical Lab Attendant to Laboratory Support at Public Health Ontario Laboratories (PHOL) has ingrained in me a deep understanding of data integrity, efficient workflows, and critical communication within regulated environments. This background allows me to approach Salesforce solutions not just from a technical perspective, but with a practical understanding of how they impact real-world operations, especially in healthcare.

I'm passionate about leveraging the power of Salesforce to streamline processes, enhance patient experiences, and drive measurable efficiency. Whether it's configuring intricate automations, managing complex data, or ensuring robust security, my goal is always to deliver user-friendly, compliant, and impactful CRM solutions.
